Why LoadScan

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Built specifically for appliance resellers who need to move faster without losing accuracy.

Instant sticker scanning

Point your camera at any appliance label. LoadScan reads the model number and pulls relevant data so you can stop typing and start processing.

Load-based pricing

Enter your load cost and target margin once. LoadScan distributes pricing across the entire manifest automatically — no spreadsheet required.

Live inventory dashboard

See stock value, load count, and profit metrics in one place. No more hunting through notes or spreadsheets to understand what you have on hand.

Clean load manifests

Every intake session builds a structured manifest you can review, export, or hand off — clean records from the very first scan.

Fast, offline-ready

LoadScan works where your loads are — warehouse floors, loading docks, or anywhere without strong signal. No internet required to scan and save.

Private by default

Your inventory data stays on your device. No account, no cloud sync required. Your business data is yours — LoadScan doesn't collect or sell it.
